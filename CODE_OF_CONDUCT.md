# Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose
This community is dedicated to providing a welcoming, professional, and harassment-free experience for all participants, regardless of background, identity, or level of experience. All contributors, users, and maintainers of the `isa4js` project are expected to adhere to this Code of Conduct.

## 2. Expected Standards
To foster a positive and collaborative environment, participants are expected to:

- **Demonstrate respect and empathy** toward other community members, differing viewpoints, and experiences.
- **Provide and accept constructive feedback** gracefully, focusing on technical and objective improvement.
- **Use professional language** in all project-related communications, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- **Prioritize collaboration and support** to help new contributors successfully engage with the project.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
The following behaviors are considered violations of this Code of Conduct and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment or discrimination**, including offensive comments related to personal characteristics, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, race, or religion.
- **Personal attacks**, insults, trolling, or derogatory comments.
- **Privacy violations**, such as publishing others' private information without explicit, prior permission.
- **Any other conduct** that could reasonably be considered inappropriate or unprofessional in a professional setting.

## 4. Enforcement and Reporting
Project maintainers hold the responsibility to clarify and enforce these standards of behavior.

- **Reporting**: Violations of this Code of Conduct may be reported privately by contacting the project maintainers.
- **Action**: Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Maintainers are empowered to take corrective action, which may include warning the offender, temporary suspension, or permanent exclusion from the project repository and associated spaces.
