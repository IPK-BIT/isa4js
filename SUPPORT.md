# Support Guide

Thank you for using `isa4js`! Here is how you can get help or ask questions about the library:

## ❓ General Questions
For "How do I do X?" or configuration questions, please do not open a bug issue. Instead:
* Open a **GitHub Discussion** in the Q&A category.

## 🐛 Bug Reports & Feature Requests
If you have confirmed that something is broken or have a concrete feature suggestion, please use our interactive templates under the **Issues** tab.
